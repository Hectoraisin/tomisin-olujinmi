Who Ruined Search?

Google displaced other search engines by leveraging a simple, minimalistic interface and superior search algorithm. While its competitors were cluttered, ad-ridden web portals, Google had one specific purpose and it did it well. The top search results are fluffy, long-form content ranking primarily because of the number of keywords and backlinks on the page. Despite the company’s best efforts to convince us otherwise, Google cannot reliably infer content quality.
